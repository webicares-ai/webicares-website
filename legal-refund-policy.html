Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Website Plans

Full refunds are available if a cancellation request is made before development work begins. Once design or development work has started, fees become non-refundable, as they reflect time and resources already committed to your project.

2. Marketing Services (SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads)

Management fees for the current billing month are non-refundable once campaign or SEO work has begun. Any unspent ad budget held with Google or Meta directly (not via Webicares) is separately subject to those platforms' own policies.

Renewal fees are non-refundable once a renewal payment has been processed and hosting/services have continued into the new term.
